Hotdog

Description

The classing american hotdog (no dog inside) is a staple food at sporting events and can be easily enjoyed at home.

Ingredients

  • 8 beef hotdogs
  • 8 hotdog buns

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Fill a pot with water and bring to boil.
  3. Place hotdogs in boiling water for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Turn of water and remove hot dogs from pot. Place inside hot dog buns and season with choice of toppings.
  5. Enjoy!
